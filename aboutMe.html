About Me

In this section you will find some information about myself.

Hi! I'm Sebastián Fripp

Hello, I am Sebastián Fripp, I am currently a computer engineering student in the sixth semester and working at IBM as a technical project leader for SSA + MX. My areas of interest are currently focused on software architecture, specifically in the cloud, but I also have a great interest in backend development with Node JS. The reality is that the area of artificial intelligence is not something that I would like to dedicate myself to at the moment, but it is something that catches my attention a lot. This interest comes a lot from the various artificial intelligence technologies that I have seen in my current work, and was further enhanced with the release of Watson X, IBM's new AI and Data platform.

I hope that the course continues to motivate me to investigate and understand this area, since it is one of the technologies that is booming right now and will continue to be for a long time.
